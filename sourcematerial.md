# The Garden Center

Our garden center is your destination for the finest plants, unique gifts, and expert advice. Whether you are a seasoned gardener or just getting started, our knowledgeable staff is here to help you create something beautiful.

## Annuals

Annuals provide the most color to your garden for the longest period of time. Country Nursery has a huge selection of the newest varieties as well as plenty of your old favorites. We grow most of the annuals we sell to ensure that we can offer the best quality and variety.

- Hanging baskets
- Deer resistant annuals
- Herbs and vegetables
- Proven Winners

## Perennials

At Country Nursery we sell thousands of beautiful perennials each year. We receive deliveries of new perennials at least twice a week so that we always have a great selection to choose from. Our knowledgeable staff can help you design perennials into your landscape for long lasting beauty.

- Deer resistant selections
- Flowering vines
- Plants that attract butterflies and hummingbirds
- Groundcovers

## Garden Accents and Gifts
